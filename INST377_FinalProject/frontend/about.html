Welcome to Weatherly! Our app lets you access accurate weather forecasts and maps for any city in the U.S.—quickly and easily.

Mission Statement

Weatherly was designed to give users simple and fast access to trustworthy weather updates. Whether you're commuting, traveling, or just planning your outfit, Weatherly has your back.

How It Works

Enter your city and state code, and Weatherly connects to OpenWeatherMap’s APIs to fetch detailed local forecasts. View conditions through engaging visual charts and save your favorite places.

Technologies Used

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript for user interface and interactivity
  • OpenWeatherMap API for real-time weather data
  • Chart.js for clean, informative visuals
